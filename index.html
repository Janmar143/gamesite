Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. The two opposing teams fight to reach and destroy the enemy's base while defending their own base for control of a path, the three "lanes" known as "top", "middle" and "bottom", which connects the bases. In each team, there are five players who each control an avatar, known as a "hero", from their own device. Weaker computer-controlled characters, called "minions", spawn at team bases and follow the three lanes to the opposite team's base, fighting enemies and turrets.

The qualifier for the Mobile Legends Professional League Philippines has officially concluded with a profusion of Filipino Mobile Legends: Bang Bang hopefuls. After more than three weeks of vying for a regular season slot, 10 of the country’s best MLBB teams have been finally decided.

On May 12, 2018, the contenders will battle it out for the massive PHP 5,000,000 prize pool and the right to be called the first national champion of the Mobile Legends Professional League Philippines.


The following 10 teams have all earned their rightful spot in the Mobile Legends Professional League PH regular season. The various players from each team hail from Quezon City to as far as Iloilo and Nueva Ecija.
